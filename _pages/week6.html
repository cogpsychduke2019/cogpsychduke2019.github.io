    Friday, June 21st - Education (#27/28 of class):

    Learning Objectives:

    Resources: Slides

    1. LO1: Continue to build a supportive classroom culture & discuss science communication.
      • Pick a topic that we have studied in class or a paper. What interests you about the topic? What were limitations in how it was studied?
      • First think to yourself on your own about this, then after ~5 min, get together in a group to discuss, then write on the board what you might study
    2. LO2: Define, identify, and apply previous constructs that we have discussed in class
      • Quiz on previous material since last quiz: multiple choice & short answers
    3. LO3: Describe the basic findings from education research on multitasking.
      • Discuss the Oppenheimer & military papers & debate policy on banning laptops
      • How does this research relate to previously discussed work on real-life multitasking? Is there a better way of thinking about multitasking in the classroom?
      • Has anyone ever experienced the effects described here? How does this relate to concepts on shared attention?
